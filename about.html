About Us

Our Mission

To empower Filipino cyclists with a smart, safe, and community-driven navigation app that enhances every ride from daily commutes to outdoor adventures.

Our Vision

To become the leading cycling navigation app in the Philippines, advocating for bike safety, accessibility, and a strong biking community.

Our Story

BikeRuta started as a school project born from a passion for cycling and a desire to make bike commuting safer and more accessible in the Philippines. Built by a group of Multimedia Arts students, our goal was simple: to give bikers a smarter way to navigate through the city and beyond.
