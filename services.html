HOW TO USE BIKERUTA

What We Offer

BikeRuta is more than just a navigation app. It’s your cycling companion on the road.

Open the App

Launch the BikeRuta app on your mobile device. Make sure your GPS is turned on for accurate navigation.

Enter Your Destination

Type in the address or place you want to go. You can search by street name, landmark, or pinned location on the map.

Choose Your Route

Select between two route options:

  • Fastest Route – Prioritizes speed and efficiency.
  • Safest Route – Prioritizes bike lanes, low-traffic areas, and rider safety.
Follow the Live Map Guidance

Start your ride and follow the turn-by-turn directions on the screen. The map will update in real time with security alerts, traffic, and weather updates.

Save and Share Your Route

After your ride, save your route for future use and share it with friends or other bikers in the BikeRuta community.
